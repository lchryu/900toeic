### 📄 Questions 131–134 (Completed Version)

> **From:** Gold Star Bank  
> **Subject:** Gold Star Bank App  
> 
> Dear Customer:
> 
> Here at Gold Star Bank, we take our customers and their needs **<u>seriously</u>** (131). As some of you know, we are experiencing technical difficulties with our mobile app. Our engineers **<u>are working</u>** (132) on this problem now.
> 
> The app is expected to be up and running **<u>within</u>** (133) the next twenty-four hours. In the meantime, banking transactions can be done at **<u>any</u>** (134) of our branch locations.

#### 📝 Vocabulary & Analysis
- **Technical difficulties:** Khó khăn/sự cố kỹ thuật.
- **Experienced:** Trải qua/gặp phải (vấn đề).
- **Software bug:** Lỗi phần mềm.
- **Up and running:** Hoạt động trở lại.
- **In the meantime:** Trong lúc chờ đợi.
- **Branch locations:** Các chi nhánh.

#### 💡 Key Takeaways:
1. The mobile app is **currently down** due to a software bug.
2. The bank expects a resolution **within 24 hours**.
3. Customers can use **physical branches** or **ATMs** for now.

### 📢 Questions 135–138 (Completed Version)

> Bethenie Industries guarantees that its products will function as **<u>advertised</u>** (135) for at least one year from date of purchase.
> 
> Products **<u>that</u>** (136) are found to be defective may be shipped to our address. Please note that products should be shipped back to us, whenever **<u>possible</u>** (137), **<u>in</u>** (138) their original packaging.

#### 📝 Vocabulary & Analysis
- **Guarantees:** Bảo đảm/Cam kết.
- **Function as advertised:** Hoạt động đúng như quảng cáo.
- **Period may be extended:** Thời hạn có thể được gia hạn.
- **Defective:** Bị lỗi/Hỏng.
- **Repair or exchange:** Sửa chữa hoặc đổi mới.
- **Original packaging:** Bao bì gốc.

#### 💡 Key Takeaways:
1. Standard warranty is **one year**, but some products have longer periods.
2. Warranty only covers items from **licensed distributors**.
3. Defective items should be returned in **original packaging** if possible.
